How It Works

Understanding APR-based stake trading

1

APR Predictions

Users stake with their predicted future APR

2

Value Calculation

Stake value = (Amount × Price × Predicted APR) / Current APR

3

Harberger Tax

5% annual tax on predicted value

4

Buy Opportunity

Purchase undervalued stakes at predicted value

Buy Stakes

Purchase stakes from other users based on their APR predictions. Lower APR predictions = cheaper stakes.
